I love to skate!

Aside from art, skating is a big passion of mine. I love to strap on my inlines and go to the rink with my friends.

Introducing Inline Skates

Inlines skates are my go-to. I used to use quad skates, but once I made the switch, I never went back. Inline skates are

  • Faster
  • More comfortable
  • More stable

Stay happy and healthy

Skating is great for your health! It is great cardio and helps build your balance and coordination. It also builds not only your leg muscles but also your upper body muscles when you turn your body to skate.

Noncommittal fun

No need for a gym membership; by buying skates, you are paying in advance for endless fun. Put them on anytime and start rolling!

Great conversation starter

You can easily meet new people and make new friends through skating. Whether you go to the skate park or skating rink, or simply mention it when listing hobbies, skating can bring people together.

Check out some popular types of skates

Different skates are made with different goals in mind. The type you buy deoends on location, terrain, and general skating style and level.

Secure the foot

The boot of the skate gives a place for your foot to go. Boots can vary in material and design, but they should fit snugly.

Connect the upper and lower half

The plate is the metal piece between the boot and the wheels. It allows you to roll without losing your wheels all over the place!

Lets roll

Arguable the most crucial part. Wheels vary in material and size, based on your personal skating goals and conditions.
